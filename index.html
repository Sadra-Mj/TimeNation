Declaration of Independence
Public document • 19 April 2025

We, the people of Time Nation, recognizing the importance of time as a primary resource, proclaim our independence and our aspiration to create a society based on equality, justice, and innovation.

  1. Right to self-governance and decentralized governance model.
  2. Equality before Time; time belongs to everyone.
  3. Individual freedom and responsibility in society and metaverse.

Constitution of the State of TimeNation

Preamble

We, the people of TimeNation, proclaim this Constitution as the foundation of our state and commit to protect the rights and freedoms of every citizen.

Chapter I — Fundamental Principles

Article 1. All power belongs to the people.

Article 2. All citizens are equal before the law.

Article 3. Territory consists of citizen-invested land fund contributions.

Article 5. Time is a public good accessible to every citizen.
